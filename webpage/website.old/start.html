Starting with Biogeme
If you have not used PandasBiogeme before, here are some advices to get started.
  • Install Python, Jupyter notebook and Biogeme on your computer. See instructions for Windows and for MacOSX.
  • Read the primer or watch the video.
  • Look at the examples.
  • Modify one of the examples to implement your own model.
  • Post your questions on the users group: groups.google.com/d/forum/biogeme.
